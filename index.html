BIO

BIO

I am a Kennesaw State University alum, with a B.S. in Communication and a M.A. in American Studies. I have more than 10 years of experience working in media, sales, and marketing positions. A little over 3 years ago, I expanded my skillset to include instructional design and teaching. In addition to working as a part-time instructor, I am also a full-time technical writer for a software company. My strengths include writing focused, task-oriented and user-oriented documentation; developing and implementing in-class and eLearning curricula; and conducting research to ensure that both my writing and instructional materials are accurate, complete, and usable. During my free time, I enjoy reading, cooking, and spending time with my family. A little-known fact about me: I love retro video games, and have an original NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) circa 1983. My two favorite games are Super Mario Brothers and Tetris.
